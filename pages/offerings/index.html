What do we offer?

Technical Roadmaps

Do you need a plan to deploy a new feature or fix? Or the correct order to build a technology that meets your business needs quickly while minimizing risk? We can help you create a plan customized to meet your unique business needs

Product Viability

We can assess your current product and pitch before discussing technical challenges you are likely to face in development, potential pitfalls, and highlight important trade-offs.

Infrastructure and Automation

We can help you automate creation and deployment of infrastructure when your team pushes new code, or make your existing infrastructure more reliable, cost- effective, and replaceable by creating custom infrastructure-as-code for you.

Solutions Architecture

We will develop one or more options for software combinations that meet your business and product needs. Solutions architecture is useful for planning new features or products, sales pitches, or as part of a plan to address shortcomings in your current platform

Data and Database Design

We can plan out the best way to store and access data for your business requirements, from the earliest versions into the future.

Software Development

We offer expert development in Python, Typescript, Javascript, Remix, React, Next.js, and the flexibility to work with the programming languages and tools you are already using.
